Game Overview

Count Masters is an exciting stickman runner game that combines strategy with fast-paced action. Your mission is to guide a growing army of stickmen through various obstacles while collecting new recruits and avoiding deadly traps.

As you progress through each level, you'll need to make quick decisions about which paths to take, when to split your army, and how to maximize your forces. The game features intuitive swipe controls that make it easy to play on both desktop and mobile devices.

  • Colorful 3D graphics and smooth animations
  • Multiple challenging levels and obstacles
  • Easy-to-learn swipe controls
  • Free to play with no downloads required
  • Compatible with all modern browsers
  • Mobile-optimized for touch devices

How to Play

Playing Count Masters is simple but mastering it requires skill and strategy. Use your mouse or finger to guide your stickman army left and right, avoiding obstacles that can reduce your numbers.

Look for multiplication gates that can double or triple your army size. The more stickmen you have, the better your chances of surviving the final battle at the end of each level. Plan your moves carefully and watch your army grow!

Tips & Strategies

Success in Count Masters requires both quick reflexes and strategic thinking. Always aim for the highest multiplication gates when possible, but be careful not to lead your army into deadly obstacles.

Pay attention to the patterns of moving obstacles and time your movements accordingly. Remember that sometimes it's better to take a smaller multiplication to avoid losing your entire army. The key is finding the right balance between risk and reward.
